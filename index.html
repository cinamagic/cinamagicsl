Our Story
Born From Passion,
Built for Cinema
🎬
2012
Founded

CinaMagic Inc. is Sierra Leone's leading film production company, dedicated to telling authentic African stories with cinematic excellence. From concept to final cut, we bring imagination to life on screen.

Our team of directors, producers, actors, and technicians collaborate to create films, documentaries, and commercial content that resonate with local and global audiences alike.

  • Full-scale feature film production & direction
  • Professional talent development & training
  • Documentary & commercial video production
  • Post-production, editing & visual effects
  • Film distribution & audience engagement
